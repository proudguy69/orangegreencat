# Modlist of OGCSMP

As of right now OGC uses the following mods, feel free to add them to your mod folder

---
**ALL MODS USE LATEST VERSION OF FABRIC LOADER AND ARE FOR 1.21.11**

__*All mods below are links and you can click them__

*All mods with an * are required*

- [*OGCSMP 0.4.0](https://modrinth.com/mod/ogcsmp)
  - The main mod for the OGC SMP Server, this updates the most often

- [*Biomes O' Plenty](https://modrinth.com/mod/biomes-o-plenty)
  - Adds 50+ Biomes to the world

- [*GlitchCore](https://modrinth.com/mod/glitchcore)
  - Required dependancy of Biomes O' Pleanty

- [*TerraBlender](https://modrinth.com/mod/terrablender)
  - Another required dependancy of the above

- [*Serene Seasons](https://modrinth.com/mod/serene-seasons)
  - Adds seasons to minecraft

- [AppleSkin](https://modrinth.com/mod/appleskin?version=1.21.11&loader=fabric)
  - Adds food/hunger information to the HUD

- [Sodium](https://modrinth.com/mod/sodium)
  - Highly recomended optimzation mod

- [*Geckolib](https://modrinth.com/mod/geckolib)
  - Gives developers the ability to make custom animations

- [*Simple VC](https://modrinth.com/plugin/simple-voice-chat)
  - Adds voice chat to minecraft

- [Shulker Box Tooltip](https://modrinth.com/mod/shulkerboxtooltip)
  - View the contents of shulker boxes from your inventory
