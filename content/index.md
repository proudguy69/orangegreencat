# 📜 General Conduct

1.  **Be Respectful:** Harassment, hate speech, or excessive trolling will result in an immediate ban. Treat others as you wish to be treated.
2.  **No Griefing:** Do not destroy or modify other players' builds without explicit permission. *Minor pranks are allowed if they are reversible and non-destructive.*
3.  **Language:** Keep chat civil. Excessive swearing or spamming is not allowed.

# 🚫 Game Exploits

* **No Hacking/Cheating:** The use of any external program (like X-ray, Aimbot, etc.) to gain an unfair advantage is strictly forbidden.
* **Minor Exploits:** Minor vanilla bugs or exploits (like simple duping mechanisms) may be allowed only if explicitly approved by an admin.

# 🏘️ Base and Land

* **Claiming Land:** Land claiming is generally first-come, first-served. If you build near an established base, be friendly and introduce yourself.
* **The Nether/End:** Keep common travel areas clean and usable for everyone.
